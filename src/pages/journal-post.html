The Beauty of Tension Set Diamond Engagement Rings with MDTdesign Melbourne

Tension set diamond engagement rings epitomize modern elegance and timeless allure. At MDTdesign Melbourne, our award-winning jewellers handmake these exquisite pieces with precision and passion, ensuring each ring is a true masterpiece. This blog delves into the captivating world of tension set rings, the meticulous handmade jewellery process at MDTdesign, and the numerous benefits of choosing a tension set design for your engagement ring.

The Art of Tension Set Diamond Engagement Rings | What is a Tension Set Ring?

A tension set ring holds the diamond in place using the tension of the metal band, creating the illusion that the diamond is floating. This design showcases the diamond’s brilliance without the need for traditional prongs or bezels, allowing maximum light to enter the stone from all angles.

Why Choose Tension Set Rings?

Tension set rings are celebrated for their unique, modern aesthetic and the way they enhance the natural beauty of the diamond.

Here are some key benefits:

  • Unique and Modern Design: Tension set rings offer a sleek, contemporary look that stands out from traditional designs.
  • Enhanced Diamond Brilliance: With no prongs or bezels obstructing the view, more light can enter the diamond, enhancing its sparkle and brilliance.
  • Low Profile: Tension set rings often sit closer to the finger, providing a more comfortable and secure fit..
  • Durability: Handmaking our tension set design ensure that the metal securely holds the diamond in place, ensuring it remains protected.

Cultivating a Tension Set Engagement Ring: The Handmade Jewellery Process at MDTdesign

Commitment to Craftsmanship

MDTdesign Melbourne is dedicated to creating handmade jewellery without any mass production. Each tension set engagement ring is handmade by skilled artisans using traditional handmaking techniques combined with modern innovations.

Design Development

Using the client’s input, the design is developed by viewing a vast range featured in our Tension Set engagement ring collection. This way our designers can get an understanding about what designs you like and why and incorporate several designs together to create something entirely unique for you. Or simply find you the perfect existing design and tailor the details to you in the material selection phase. This collaborative phase ensures that every detail aligns with the client’s vision.

Material Selection

High-quality materials are selected to create the tension set ring. The choice of metal and diamond or gemstone is crucial for achieving the desired look and durability.

Precision Crafting

Our artisans meticulously handmake each ring, carefully shaping the metal and instilling strength and resilience into it in order to hold the diamond securely using tension. This process requires exceptional skill and precision, as the diamond must be held firmly without traditional settings; a technique that MDTdesign have mastered, making them the worlds leaders in Tension Set Designs.

Quality Assurance

Every tension set ring and all jewellery at MDTdesign undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets MDTdesign’s high standards. This is why we at MDTdesign back all of our jewellery with a lifetime manufacturer’s warranty.

Conclusion

Tension set diamond engagement rings from MDTdesign Melbourne are a perfect blend of modern elegance and timeless beauty. The unique setting enhances the diamond’s brilliance, while the handmade process ensures exceptional quality and durability. By choosing a tension set ring from MDTdesign, you are selecting a piece that is not only beautiful but also a true work of art.
